Hello there! I'm Hugo, a French transplant finding my footing amidst the sunny landscapes of California. Hailing from the bustling streets of Paris, I've embarked on an exciting journey to the University of California, Riverside, where the palm trees sway and the opportunities seem boundless. My fascination with coding and computing science has been a guiding force in my life for as long as I can remember. From tinkering with my first lines of code on a clunky old computer to delving into complex algorithms, the world of technology has always felt like home to me. Growing up in a city that breathes innovation, I found myself drawn to the endless possibilities that coding offers, from creating sleek websites to crafting intricate software solutions.

Arriving at UCR, I am greeted by a vibrant community of scholars and tech enthusiasts, each with their unique perspectives and passions. The buzzing energy of the campus fuels my excitement as I dive headfirst into my computer science courses. From introductory programming languages to advanced data structures, every lecture is a step closer to mastering the intricate dance of bits and bytes. Beyond the confines of the classroom, I eagerly seek out opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills. Whether it's attending hackathons to collaborate on innovative projects or joining student organizations dedicated to fostering a love for technology, I am constantly pushing myself to grow and evolve as a coder.

But it's not all about ones and zeros. Amidst the sea of lines of code, I find moments to soak in the beauty of my new surroundings. From weekend hikes in the nearby mountains to strolls along the palm-lined streets, California's natural wonders never cease to amaze me. And let's not forget the vibrant cultural scene, where I can indulge in everything from art exhibitions to live music performances. As I navigate this new chapter of my life, I am filled with a sense of anticipation for the adventures that lie ahead. From honing my coding skills to forging lifelong friendships, each day brings with it new possibilities and opportunities for growth. And as the sun sets over the horizon, casting a warm glow over the campus, I can't help but feel grateful for the chance to pursue my passions in such a dynamic and welcoming environment.
